OUR STORY

86 Attire is a brand created by Troy Gordon. It is designed for those willing to risk the public eye staring at them and inquiring about what they are wearing. The term 86'd in the nightlife scene is meant to be someone who is permanently banned or kicked out from a venue. The clothing is for those willing to be considered 86'd based on their choice of fashion.
